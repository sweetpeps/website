  1. Login to your Mantal dashboard

  2. Create new users if needed and customize their permissions for their needs

  3. Insert premade menus to your Mantal dashboard, including time tables, user records, task management, and more

  5. Determine how certain permissions allow certain users to view, edit and change menus.
